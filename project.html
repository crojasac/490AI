Artificial Intelligence in Economics | Fall 2024

Project Overview

This project applies artificial intelligence and data analysis techniques to a topic in managerial economics. The goal is to explore how modern AI tools can provide new insights for firms, consumers, or policymakers.

The project combines economic reasoning with hands-on implementation, showcasing how machine learning, predictive modeling, or other AI methods can inform decision-making in a real or simulated business environment.

In addition to the technical results, the project reflects on the ethical and practical implications of AI in economic contexts.
